Remember a legend!

Alber Camus, was a guiding light that took place in the mid 20th century after the end of World War II. He helped to found and start the philosophical movement known ad Absurdism. A noted thinker and a gifted writer his works became internationally renowned. Some examples of his include the famous essay called "The Myth of Sysiphus" and the novel called "The Stranger".
